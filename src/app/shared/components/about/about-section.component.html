Our Mission

At Peace School Cambodia, our mission is to empower Cambodian children through education. We aim to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment where children can develop strong language skills in English and Khmer. Our dedicated team is passionate about providing quality education and making a positive impact in the lives of our students and their families.

Our Approach

We take a holistic approach to education, focusing on not only academic development but also the overall well-being of our students. Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning. We believe in creating a well-rounded educational experience that prepares our students for success in the 21st century.
